Welcome to the Kind Kitchen!

I believe that cooking and food bring people together and I want to share our joy and passion with you. My goal is to make a nourishing plant-based and cruelty-free lifestyle accessible to everyone. Since you are here, you are probably curious to know more about us. Let me make my favourite original soy milk and tell you more about my story.

Spoiler Alert. This will not be a vivid and touching story.

My mother is an absolute animal lover and she never hesitates to help the strayed animals out on the street. So basically, I grew up in a family with cats and dogs around. Over the years, I have built a close relationship with them. Through the interaction with animals, I have gained a greater sense of peace and compassion. I have always aware of the inhumane practices in factory farm. The more I thought about it, the less I wanted to participate in the cruelty of meat production. In one summer holiday, after watching a few documentaries about the realities of farming, I decided to get started on a plant-based diet.

I challenged myself to create recipes that would taste as good as anything I'd ever cooked in my past life without sacrificing nutrition. I realised it is just a simple change in mindset and lifestyle. I hope to share the ideas and resources in a community like this. Every step forward counts as progress. If you are looking for ways to either cut out or cut down on meat, you are in the right place. Alright, I am gonna enjoy my soy milk now. Hope you enjoy our blog and find some delicious inspiration here!
